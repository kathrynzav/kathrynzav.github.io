Tech Stuff

The dashboard provides real-time updates on student progress and flags when students might need assistance. View my presentation to learn more about the platform and how our solution addressed teacher needs.

Cybersecurity: I contributed to a proof-of-concept for a threat modeling AI bot for Pendo. My primary role involved identifying and preparing training materials for the bot, which taught me valuable skills in material formatting, tokenization, prompt engineering, and tolerance testing.
